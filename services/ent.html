ENT Care — Ear, Nose & Throat Evaluation, Endoscopy and Day-Care Procedures

Complete ENT care for ear infections, sinus issues, throat problems, voice change, snoring and dizziness. We use protocol-based diagnosis, endoscopic evaluation and personalised treatment plans for faster relief and safer outcomes.

Conditions We Treat

  • Ear pain, wax impaction, discharge (otitis media/externa)
  • Hearing loss, tinnitus (ringing), eardrum perforation
  • Blocked nose, allergic rhinitis, deviated septum
  • Sinusitis (acute/recurrent), nasal polyps, facial pressure
  • Sore throat, tonsillitis, adenoid hypertrophy (children)
  • Hoarseness/voice change, vocal cord lesions, reflux laryngitis
  • Vertigo (BPPV), imbalance; snoring and suspected sleep apnea
  • Neck swellings, thyroid/parotid evaluation (referral pathways as needed)

Diagnostics & Tests

  • ENT endoscopy: nasal endoscopy, laryngoscopy (as indicated)
  • Audiometry (hearing test) and tympanometry (middle ear function)
  • Positional tests for vertigo (Dix-Hallpike, canalith assessment)
  • Allergy evaluation and imaging (X-ray/CT) when required

Treatments & Procedures

  • Microscopic ear cleaning, wax removal; medical therapy for infections
  • Allergic rhinitis care: trigger control, nasal sprays, antihistamines
  • Sinusitis management; referral for FESS/septoplasty when indicated
  • Tonsillitis/adenoids: medical therapy; surgery advice for recurrent cases
  • Voice therapy advice; reflux precautions and medication
  • Vertigo: canalith repositioning maneuvers (e.g., Epley) for BPPV
  • Snoring/suspected OSA: sleep study referral, CPAP counselling where needed

When to Visit Emergency

  • Severe ear pain with fever, sudden hearing loss, facial weakness
  • Breathing difficulty, drooling, inability to swallow, severe sore throat
  • Nose bleed that does not stop with compression, head/neck injury

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced ENT team with endoscopy, audiometry and tympanometry access
  • Clear, stepwise plans—medical management first, surgery when necessary
  • Coordination with anesthesia, ICU and imaging for comprehensive care
  • Patient education on prevention, voice hygiene and allergy control
