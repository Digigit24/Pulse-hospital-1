Newborn, Child & Adolescent Care in Tathawade, PCMC

From day-one newborn care to teen health, our pediatric unit provides gentle, evidence-based treatment with clear guidance for parents. OPD, vaccination, growth tracking, allergy/asthma management, sick visits, and 24×7 emergency support—under one roof.

Age Groups We Cover

  • Newborns (0–28 days): jaundice, feeding support, safe discharge follow-ups
  • Infants (1–12 months): fever, cough-cold, weaning, nutrition plans
  • Toddlers & Children (1–10 years): infections, growth & development, injuries
  • Adolescents (10–18 years): hormonal changes, acne, obesity, mental well-being

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Fever, cough, cold, flu, ear & throat infections
  • Asthma, wheeze, allergy, atopic dermatitis
  • Diarrhea, vomiting, abdominal pain, dehydration
  • Nutritional deficiencies (iron, vitamin D), poor weight gain
  • Minor injuries, sprains, simple fractures; school health issues

Vaccination & Well-Child Care

  • All national & optional vaccines with proper cold-chain maintenance
  • Age-wise immunization reminders and catch-up schedules
  • Growth charts (WHO/ICMR), developmental milestone tracking
  • Dietary counseling, deworming, anemia & vitamin checks

On-Site Diagnostics

  • Paediatric blood tests, urine/stool tests, throat swabs
  • X-ray; ultrasound as indicated; rapid tests (flu, dengue, etc.)
  • Emergency triage with paediatric-appropriate protocols

When to Use Emergency (Do Not Delay)

  • Fast breathing, chest retractions, persistent wheeze
  • Convulsions, unresponsiveness, severe dehydration
  • High fever in infants <3 months, or fever with rash & lethargy
  • Accidental poisoning, severe injuries, burns

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced pediatricians with NICU/ICU backup and 24×7 emergency
  • Child-friendly OPD, painless vaccination practices, parent counselling
  • Fast diagnostics → quicker relief, shorter hospital stays
  • Transparent care, insurance assistance, follow-up reminders
