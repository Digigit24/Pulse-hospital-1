Best Gynecologist in Tathawade, PCMC — Pregnancy, Period Problems & Preventive Care

Compassionate, evidence-based women’s health—from teenage concerns to pregnancy care and post-menopause. We serve Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad with ethical treatment and clear guidance.

Conditions We Treat

  • Irregular periods, heavy bleeding (menorrhagia), painful periods
  • PCOS/PCOD, thyroid-related menstrual issues, acne/hair fall linked to hormones
  • Vaginal infections, urinary infections, pelvic pain, dyspareunia
  • Fibroids, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, adenomyosis
  • Infertility basic evaluation and couple counselling
  • Perimenopause & menopause symptom management

Antenatal & Postnatal Care

  • Trimester-wise ANC visits, nutrition & supplements, anomaly scans guidance
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring (GDM, hypertension, thyroid, twins)
  • Fetal growth tracking, kick-count education, labour preparation
  • Post-delivery recovery, lactation support, contraception counselling

Diagnostics Available

  • Ultrasound (obstetric & gynec), Doppler as advised
  • Pap smear, HPV testing, colposcopy referral when indicated
  • Hormonal profile: TSH, Prolactin, AMH, LH/FSH, estradiol
  • Infection workup, anemia panel, vitamin D/B12, diabetes screening

Treatments & Procedures

  • Medical management for PCOS, menstrual disorders, infections
  • Office procedures: Pap smear, endometrial sampling (where applicable)
  • Family planning: counselling, IUCD/implant advice, spacing methods
  • Minimally invasive surgery referrals: hysteroscopy, laparoscopy (fibroid/cyst)
  • Normal delivery/C-section support with NICU backup via partner facilities (as indicated)

When to Visit Emergency (Do Not Delay)

  • Severe lower-abdominal pain, fainting, or shoulder pain (possible ectopic)
  • Heavy vaginal bleeding or clots, soaking pads every hour
  • Early pregnancy bleeding, severe vomiting with dehydration
  • Late-pregnancy: reduced fetal movements, leaking fluid, severe headache/blurred vision with high BP
