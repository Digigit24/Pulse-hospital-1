Gastroenterology in Tathawade, PCMC — Endoscopy, Colonoscopy & Liver Clinic

We diagnose and treat digestive and liver disorders with clear guidance, on-site endoscopy services, and coordinated care. From acidity and IBS to pancreatitis and GI bleeding, our team offers timely evaluation, medical therapy, and interventional procedures with 24×7 emergency and ICU backup.

Conditions We Treat

  • Acidity, GERD, gastritis, peptic ulcer, H. pylori
  • Abdominal pain, gas/bloating, constipation, diarrhoea, IBS
  • Ulcerative colitis, Crohn’s disease (IBD) — flares & maintenance
  • Jaundice, hepatitis (A–E), fatty liver/NAFLD, alcoholic liver disease
  • Gallbladder stones, biliary colic, bile duct stones (choledocholithiasis)
  • Acute/chronic pancreatitis, pancreatic pain syndromes
  • GI bleeding (vomiting blood, black stools), anaemia due to occult bleed
  • Swallowing difficulty, celiac disease, small intestinal bacterial overgrowth (SIBO)

Diagnostics & On-Site Tests

  • Upper GI Endoscopy (EGD): For reflux, ulcers, bleed, celiac, strictures; biopsy as indicated
  • Colonoscopy: For bleeding, chronic diarrhoea, IBD, polyps; polypectomy/biopsy
  • ERCP (case-selected): For bile duct stones/strictures; stenting where required
  • Abdominal USG; CT/MRCP referrals; liver panel, pancreatic enzymes, H. pylori testing

Procedures & Treatments

  • Medical therapy for GERD/ulcers with lifestyle & diet counselling
  • Endoscopic control of bleed (injection/clip/band ligation as indicated)
  • Stricture dilatation, foreign body removal (case-based)
  • IBD protocols: induction & maintenance, vaccination & nutrition guidance
  • Liver care: antiviral/etiology-specific therapy, abstinence & nutrition plans
  • Pancreatitis care: pain, fluids, nutrition; management of complications

Liver Clinic & Metabolic Care

  • NAFLD/fatty liver reversal plans: weight, diabetes, lipid control
  • Alcohol-related liver disease counselling and relapse prevention
  • Cirrhosis monitoring: ascites, encephalopathy, varices screening & prophylaxis

Diet & Lifestyle Guidance

  • GERD diet, small frequent meals, trigger avoidance, sleep posture tips
  • IBS routines (FODMAP-style guidance), hydration, fibre titration
  • Hepatic nutrition: protein goals, salt restriction where needed, vaccination advice

When to Visit the Emergency

  • Vomiting blood, black/tarry stools, fresh blood per rectum
  • Severe abdominal pain with fever/vomiting, jaundice with confusion
  • Persistent vomiting or dehydration, inability to swallow liquids

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced gastroenterology team with endoscopy suite and ICU support
  • Evidence-based protocols for bleeding, IBD, pancreatitis, and liver disease
  • Integrated care with surgery, radiology, dietetics, and critical care
  • Cashless/TPA assistance and clear follow-up pathways
