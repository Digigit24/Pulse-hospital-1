Nephrology — Complete Kidney Care & Dialysis Support

Comprehensive kidney evaluation, early detection, and long-term management for AKI, CKD, hypertension, stones, infections, and transplant-related needs. On-site dialysis with ICU backup for patients across Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Conditions We Treat

  • Acute kidney injury (AKI), chronic kidney disease (CKD stages 1–5)
  • Diabetic & hypertensive nephropathy, glomerular diseases
  • Kidney stones, recurrent UTIs, pyelonephritis
  • Electrolyte & acid–base disorders, edema, proteinuria/hematuria
  • Post-transplant follow-up (in coordination with transplant centers)

Diagnostics

  • Renal function tests, urine microscopy, protein/albumin checks
  • Electrolytes, ABG, ultrasound KUB with doppler as indicated
  • Autoimmune & serology panels for specific nephropathies

Treatments & Services

  • Hemodialysis with strict infection-control and nephrologist supervision
  • Vascular access: AV fistula coordination/maintenance
  • Medication & diet plans tailored to CKD stage and comorbidities
  • Blood pressure & diabetes optimisation to slow CKD progression
  • Transplant evaluation, counselling, and continuity care

When to Visit Emergency

  • Sudden breathlessness, reduced urine, severe swelling
  • Hyperkalemia symptoms: palpitations, muscle weakness
  • Uremic symptoms: confusion, vomiting, severe fatigue

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced nephrology team with 24×7 Emergency & ICU
  • Dialysis, diagnostics, and specialist backup under one roof
  • Patient-first counselling, insurance guidance, planned follow-ups
