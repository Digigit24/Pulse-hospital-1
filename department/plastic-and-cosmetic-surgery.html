Plastic & Cosmetic Surgery — Reconstructive Precision, Aesthetic Confidence

From trauma reconstruction and burns to cosmetic enhancements, our plastic surgeons deliver safe, ethical, and personalized care with anaesthesia and ICU support. Serving Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

What We Treat

  • Post-trauma soft tissue & bone exposure, tendon/nerve injuries
  • Burns (acute care & contracture release), complex wounds
  • Hand injuries, replantation referral, microvascular repairs
  • Congenital anomalies (cleft, ear, hand anomalies)
  • Cosmetic concerns: face, body, breast shaping

Diagnostics & Planning

  • Clinical evaluation, photographic assessment, scar grading
  • Imaging & lab workup as indicated; realistic outcome counselling

Procedures

  • Skin grafts, local/regional flaps, free-flap referrals
  • Scar revision, keloid treatment protocols
  • Rhinoplasty, otoplasty, blepharoplasty
  • Liposuction, abdominoplasty (tummy tuck), body contouring
  • Gynecomastia correction; breast augmentation/reduction/lift

Recovery & Safety

  • Anaesthesia safety standards, DVT prophylaxis, infection control
  • Detailed aftercare plan: dressings, activity, sun/skin care

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Board-certified plastic surgeons with multi-specialty support
  • Confidential counselling, customised plans, ethical practices
  • 24×7 Emergency & ICU backup for complex reconstructions
