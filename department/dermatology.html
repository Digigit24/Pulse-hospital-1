Dermatology in Tathawade, PCMC — Expert Skin, Hair & Nail Care

From acne to psoriasis, infections to allergies, hair fall to nail disorders—our dermatology team offers medical, surgical, and aesthetic solutions with accurate diagnosis, clear guidance, and safe procedures. Care extends to adults, teens, and children, supported by our 24×7 hospital ecosystem.

Conditions We Treat

  • Acne, post-acne marks & scars, open pores, oily/combination skin
  • Eczema/dermatitis, urticaria (hives), contact allergies, photodermatitis
  • Psoriasis, lichen planus, vitiligo (leucoderma), melasma, pigmentation
  • Fungal, bacterial, and viral skin infections (ringworm, impetigo, warts, molluscum)
  • Hair fall, dandruff/seborrheic dermatitis, alopecia areata, male/female pattern hair loss
  • Nail disorders: brittle nails, fungal nail, ingrown nail
  • Pediatric skin: atopic dermatitis, birthmarks, insect bite allergies

Dermatology Diagnostics

  • Clinical dermoscopy for pigmented lesions, hair/scalp assessment (trichoscopy)
  • Skin scrapings, KOH mount, Wood’s lamp exam; biopsy when indicated
  • Allergy evaluation guidance and patch-test referral pathways

Medical & Procedural Treatments

  • Acne Protocols: Topicals/orals, chemical peels, scar management plan
  • Allergy/Eczema: Trigger control, emollient/anti-inflammatory regimen, flare plans
  • Psoriasis/Vitiligo: Step-wise therapy; phototherapy referral where suitable
  • Infections: Precise antimicrobials + hygiene counselling to prevent recurrence
  • Hair: Nutritional correction, minoxidil/finasteride (as eligible), PRP, microneedling
  • Nails: Medical therapy; avulsion/ingrown nail procedures when needed

Dermatosurgery & Aesthetic Dermatology

  • Electrocautery/Radiofrequency for warts, skin tags, molluscum
  • Chemical peels (acne, tan, pigmentation), microdermabrasion, microneedling
  • Scar revision planning; PRP for face/hair; under-eye protocols (case-based)
  • Laser referrals for hair reduction, pigmentation, vascular lesions (case selection)

Skin-Care Education & Prevention

  • Daily routine by skin type (cleanser–moisturiser–sunscreen guidance)
  • Diet & lifestyle tips for acne, dandruff, and eczema control
  • Sun protection habits and safe product use

When to Seek Urgent Care

  • Rapidly spreading rashes with fever, painful blisters, facial swelling, or eye involvement
  • Severe drug allergies, angioedema, or signs of secondary infection

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced dermatology team with access to multi-specialty backup
  • Evidence-based, skin-type specific protocols with clear after-care
  • Transparent counselling on medical vs cosmetic options and expected results
  • Convenient follow-ups and 24×7 emergency support
