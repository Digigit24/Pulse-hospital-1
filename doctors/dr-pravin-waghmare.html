About Dr. Pravin Waghmare

Dr. Pravin Waghmare is a Joint Replacement Surgeon, Spine & Pain Specialist, and Orthopedic Surgeon based in Pimple Saudagar, Pune, with 19 years of experience. He consults at Jeevan Jyoti Superspeciality Hospital (Pimple Saudagar), Varad Joint & Spine Clinic (Wakad), and Pawana Hospital (Talegaon). He completed MBBS from BJMC Pune (2006) and DNB – Orthopedics/Orthopedic Surgery from National Board of Examination (2011). He is a member of the Indian Medical Association (IMA) and the National Academy of Medical Sciences.

  • Deformity assessment and corrective Orthopedics.
  • Total/partial Joint Replacement surgery (knee, hip, shoulder).
  • Spinal Disc Surgery, Spondylosis care, Spinal Fusion planning.
  • Treatment for Heel pain and comprehensive Joint Pain management.

Professional Skills

Primary & revision joint replacements, arthroplasty planning, spine disorder evaluation, pain protocols, rehabilitation-focused recovery, and evidence-based orthopaedic care.

Education & Credentials

Formal training with national boards and active membership in reputed medical associations.

  • MBBS (2006) – BJMC Pune.
  • DNB Orthopedics (2011) – National Board of Examination.
  • Memberships – Indian Medical Association (IMA); National Academy of Medical Sciences.

Conditions & Care

Structured diagnosis, surgical and non-surgical options, and post-operative rehab to restore movement and reduce pain.

  • Osteoarthritis, sports injuries, ligament & meniscus issues.
  • Back/neck pain, disc prolapse, nerve compression symptoms.
  • Foot/ankle pain including plantar fasciitis & heel pain.
  • Fracture management, joint dislocation, and trauma care.
