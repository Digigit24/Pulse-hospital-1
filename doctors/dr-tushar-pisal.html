About Dr. Tushar Pisal

Dr. Tushar Pisal is a Spine Surgeon (Ortho) and Orthopaedic surgeon in Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune with 19 years of experience. He consults at Dhanashree Hospital (PCMC), Pawana Hospital (Talegaon) and Painex Pain Management Clinic (Deccan Gymkhana, Pune). He completed MBBS (2006) and MS – Orthopaedics (2012) from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences. He is registered with the Maharashtra Medical Council.

  • Spinal disorders: neck, back pain and radiculopathy evaluation.
  • Spinal disc surgery: herniated disc, decompression procedures.
  • Minimally invasive spine surgery with faster recovery focus.
  • Spinal therapy & bracing with rehab-oriented care plans.

Professional Skills

Comprehensive spine assessment, imaging-guided decision making, microdiscectomy and MIS techniques, post-operative rehabilitation protocols and brace optimisation for stability and pain relief.

Education & Credentials

Strong academic foundation with focused orthopaedic and spine training; active clinical practice across multi-specialty centres in Pune region.

  • MBBS (2006) – Maharashtra University of Health Sciences.
  • MS – Orthopaedics (2012) – Maharashtra University of Health Sciences.
  • Registration – Maharashtra Medical Council.

Conditions & Care

Personalised spine care with conservative to surgical pathways; emphasis on pain reduction, function, and safe return to activity.

  • Cervical/lumbar spondylosis, sciatica and stenosis management.
  • Disc prolapse evaluation, microdiscectomy/MIS options.
  • Posture correction, physiotherapy protocols and bracing.
  • Second opinions for spine surgery and recovery planning.
