Dr. Mahesh Jadhav

Dr. Mahesh Jadhav is a trusted and one of the best General Physicians in Ravet and Pimpri Chinchwad. A well-known MD (Medicine) and Diabetologist serving Ravet, Kiwale, Punawale, Nigdi, and wider PCMC, he has 14 years of experience in Internal Medicine. He currently practices at Atharvam Diagnostics & Polyclinic, Ravet, Pune. He completed DNB – General Medicine from MGM Medical College, Aurangabad (2010) and MBBS from MUHS, Nashik (2004).

  • Fever, infections, dengue, malaria & typhoid evaluation and treatment.
  • Diabetes care: diagnosis, medication titration, diet & lifestyle planning.
  • Hypertension, cholesterol & thyroid disorder management.
  • General health checkups, preventive screenings & second opinions.

Professional Skills

Internal Medicine expertise with strong focus on diabetes, blood pressure, infections, and multi-system disorders. Emphasis on clear diagnosis, safe medicines, patient education, and long-term disease prevention.

General Medicine Specialist

Evidence-based medical care for acute illnesses and chronic conditions with holistic follow-up plans. From persistent fever and infections to diabetes, hypertension, lipid and thyroid disorders — comprehensive care tailored to your health goals.

  • DNB – General Medicine (2010) – MGM Medical College, Aurangabad.
  • MBBS (2004) – Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik.
  • Diabetology Focus – Recognized experience in diabetes prevention and control programs.

Conditions & Care

Consult for accurate diagnosis, rational medicines, lifestyle guidance, and structured follow-ups to stay healthy and active.

  • Acute care: viral fever, flu, throat & chest infections.
  • Chronic care: diabetes, BP, cholesterol, thyroid, obesity.
  • Preventive health: annual checkups, cardiac risk screening.
  • Second opinions & medication optimization.
