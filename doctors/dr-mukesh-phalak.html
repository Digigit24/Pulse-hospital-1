Dr. Mukesh Phalak

Dr. Mukesh Phalak is a Spine Surgeon and Trauma Surgeon based in Nigdi, Pune with 12 years of experience. He practices at Omkar Clinic, Nigdi. He completed MS – Orthopaedics from Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College (LTMMC), Sion, Mumbai in 2007; DNB – Orthopedics/Orthopedic Surgery from National Board in 2010; and AFIH from LTMMC, Sion, Mumbai in 2014. His clinical work covers bone & joint disorders, spinal disc disease, spondylosis, spinal fusion and joint dislocation treatment.

  • Bone & joint treatment for pain, arthritis and injuries.
  • Spinal disc surgery and minimally invasive decompression.
  • Spondylosis management (neck & back) with rehab guidance.
  • Spinal fusion and joint dislocation reduction & care.

Professional Skills

Orthopedic trauma, spine surgery, joint preservation and post-operative rehabilitation with focus on early mobility and return to activity.

Orthopedics & Spine Specialist

Evidence-based care for fractures, ligament injuries, cervical/lumbar spine problems and degenerative joint disease—personalized to pain relief and function.

  • MS – Orthopaedics (2007) – Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College, Sion, Mumbai.
  • DNB – Orthopedics (2010) – National Board of Examinations.
  • AFIH (2014) – LTMMC, Sion, Mumbai.

Conditions & Care

Visit for precise diagnosis, structured treatment and guided physiotherapy to restore strength and mobility.

  • Back & neck pain, slipped disc, sciatica, spondylosis.
  • Fractures, sports injuries, ligament & tendon tears.
  • Shoulder, knee and hip pain; arthritis management.
  • Post-trauma and post-surgery rehabilitation plans.
