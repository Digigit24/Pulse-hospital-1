About Dr. Sunil Palve

Dr. Sunil Palve is a renowned Urologist in Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad with vast expertise in urological diseases and advanced urosurgery. He treats kidney and genitourinary stones, prostate enlargement & prostate cancer, genitourinary cancers, urinary tract infections, and male sexual health problems with evidence-based protocols and minimally invasive techniques.

  • Comprehensive stone care: URS, RIRS (flexible ureteroscopy), PCNL & laser lithotripsy.
  • Prostate treatment: medical therapy, TURP / HoLEP based on indication.
  • Uro-oncology: kidney, bladder, prostate & testicular cancers—evaluation to treatment planning.
  • Andrology & men’s health: ED, infertility evaluation, Peyronie’s & hormonal assessment.

Professional Skills

Endourology, laparoscopy, laser stone surgery, prostate surgery (TURP/HoLEP), urethral reconstruction, uro-oncology work-ups, day-care protocols and rapid recovery pathways.

Urology Specialist

Personalized urology care with clear diagnosis, accurate imaging, safe anesthesia practices and step-wise treatment—aimed at pain relief, early discharge and long-term outcomes.

  • Advanced Endourology – Laser RIRS/PCNL programs for complex renal & ureteric stones.
  • Prostate Expertise – Medical to surgical management including TURP/HoLEP pathways.
  • Uro-Oncology – Multidisciplinary evaluation for kidney, bladder & prostate cancers.

Conditions & Care

Consult for stone pain, urinary symptoms, infections, post-surgery follow-ups and men’s health concerns.

  • Kidney/ureteric stones: diagnosis, laser fragmentation, stent management.
  • Prostate enlargement (BPH): LUTS assessment, PSA guidance, surgery when needed.
  • Recurrent UTI, hematuria, urinary obstruction & stricture disease.
  • Male sexual problems: erectile dysfunction, infertility evaluation & counseling.
